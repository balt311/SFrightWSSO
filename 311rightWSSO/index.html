Single Sign On is HERE for CITY 311 USERS!!!  Those with baltimorecity.gov accounts and a 311/Salesforce license should use the 'Or log in: using Baltimore City Login'  and follow the City authentication steps to access the 311 Console.  No more having to use/remember/reset your Salesforce password! Contact 311 support if any issues.
  ▶ NON-CITY USERS please continue to log in with your 311/Salesforce credentials however you will need to download & configure the Salesforce Authenticator App ( apple | android) in order to 2-step verify your login.

 311 license account requests should be made through the 311 System
